ARTIC - BLOG

Abstract
In the current scenario, people do look out for different kind of experiences for the tasks they do, as it is found to be exciting to gather new knowledge about the world we live in. Where such tasks take place, approximately everyone looks out to something for sharing their experiences with the world. And blog is such a medium, which allows people to share information and knwoledge about such experiences with the entire world, using virtual technology.
The trend for Blog application has some very major vendors currently existing in the business, like BlogSpot, Wordpress, Medium, Tumblr, and many more.


The graphical data displays that humans from all age groups are more attracted towards writing the blogs, and sharing their opinions, views, or sharing information with the world.

Among this population, lies several bloggers, who are not able to capture much of the public attention, even if they appear to be great writers. This happens, bacuse categorically, only the top rated blogs are viewed by the users eye. And top-rated blogs are written generally by top-rated bloggers. So the population for such bloggers restricts to limit the community for biased views for any category or topic. And if someone wishes to be rated among the top, and is a great writer too, most cases do show that such people are really afraid of exposing themselves to the world, by the means of many social media platforms, and tend to lose out the scope of writing. This way, we lose approximately a hundred great writers each.


Introduction
Artic - Blog is a non-biased, public exposure, social media platform, on which, people can express their feelings and there opinions on different views and presence reports for occasions, for interaction, as well as for sharing their thoughts by this medium. Artic-Blog clearly displays the most recent published articles, or any other form of write-up for topics that were been displayed as a text for that topic. This application also provides its users a method to create articles, and share their own experience with the world. For writing articles, the users only need to login with the name they wish to be displayed to the world as their identitiy, and a few basic personal details like age, gender, and such information. After such registration, the user os free to write as many articles as he or she desires to and make the most of the application.


Literature Review
The concept of this application has been extracted from two different existing domains. One being Blogspot.com and Wordpress.com, which are the current leaders for creating blogs and articles for publishing and making it available for view to the world. These applications displayed the necessary information that needs to be taken from any user for feeding into the application for perusal to other users. Also, they explained the data flow of how the data needs to be taken and how it needs to be made available to the people around the globe.
The other application which is responsible for the creation of this application is Duck-Duck Go browser. This application too holds an important role, as this browser inspired us to make unbiased choice for visibility of users to all write-ups, rather than several write-ups. Restrictedd access to most popular posts generally deny a varied access to any viewer as well as to the authors for such writers. Un-Biased views to choice is what is mostly appreciated by humans these days, as well as guarantees the author more views for their posts.

    ARTIC - DEVOPS

    Abstract
    Developer Operations is an important domain in the flow of any business. General description about any devOps domain specifies the communication between officals and individuals by the means of information, tools, and collaborative work experiences. Such individuals generally specify that these kind of collaborative work experience enhances productive attitude of any project workflow. Several projects exists, which require team collaboration with general uptime to any communication platform. It can be individual sometimes, to which the needs can be fulfilled by simple telephonic conversation, but for large teams, the need for a collaborative nature is required to make the most out of any project. Also for major updates, requiring immediate action on any problem that might arise, there is the need for a platform for communication between the team to resolve the probelms at ease and make the task available to any customer.


    Introduction
    Artic - DevOps is an application which can be easily utilised for communication between different team members engaged for a project to validate and share information between the team members, as well as maintain record for several conversations that has taken place between the team. DevOps is actually an API provision for integrating among different platforms for ease of access to projects planning to integrate such an idea to personalise their experience on the platform. Several projects looking for monitoring of their projects by modes of multimedia communication can look forward at Artic-DevOps API for advancement.
